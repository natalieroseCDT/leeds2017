About Me

I am a PhD student on the CDT for Data Analytics and Society course, based in the Geographic Data Science Lab at the University of Liverpool. Although based in Liverpool, the course encompasses study across the Universities of Leeds, Manchester and Sheffield and their respective data science institutes.

My main PhD project focuses on the impact of weather on high street retail and consumer behaviour, specifically the types of products being purchased.

Other current areas of study include:

  • Programming for Social Scientists
  • Social Survey Analysis
  • Qualitative Research Methods
  • Geographic Information Science
